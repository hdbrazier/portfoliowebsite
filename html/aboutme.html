A little about me

I was born in England and raised in Spain, on the mediterranean coast in a town called Javea. I learnt to sail at the yatch club which in turn introduced me to scuba diving. As soon as I turned 18, I jumped on a plane and moved to the Cayman islands to teach diving. I always knew I was a little different and was always searching for a new thrill. At the age of 22, I moved to the US, where I settled in Florida. It was here that I enrolled in a flight school and learned how to fly helicopters.

I have ran multiple flight schools, teaching people how to fly, I flew for the news channel and then I moved to Las Vegas where I flew tours to the Grand Canyon. The last 2 years, I have been living in Indianapolis where I work for St Vincent Hospital group, flying EMS. Although the schedule can be demanding, it is a great job where I know I am helping people for good.

I am not sure what the future holds for me, I am looking to expand my knowledge and learn a new skill, programming has become a new part of my life and I expect great things to come of it.

I have a little dog named Nyx, she is a corgi mix that looks like a Jack Russel Terrier. She is 11 years old now although that hasn't slowed her down at all. I adopted her off the street when she was just 3 weeks old and she has travelled with me since.

Charlie is my 8 year old cat, I rescued her from a hoarding situation when I lived in Florida, where she was bullied continuosly by the other cats. She is extremely loving and demands attention, just not the belly scratches.

The newest addition to my little animal family is Pepper, she was found abandoned in the street and I recently discovered she is pregnant.
